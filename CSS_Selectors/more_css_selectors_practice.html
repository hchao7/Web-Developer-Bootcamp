KAYDEN

Introduction

Kayden (카이든 Kai-deun) or more commonly known as Kayden Break (카이든 브레이크 Kai-deun Beu-lei-keu [Kaiden Bureiku]) is the deuteragonist in the manhwa, Eleceed. He is notorious as one of the strongest unaffiliated awakeners who challenges the strong. Due to being heavily injured and needing to hide as he healed, he was forced to transform into a cat and can only maintain his human form by spending his energy.

Powers

As one of the most powerful awakeners in the world, Kayden has a long experience fighting other awakeners. Unlike most awakeners, Kayden never received any form of training and is entirely self-taught. He was able to create a high-level force control for himself and so far, he has taught this method to Jiwoo only.

Personality

Kayden is passionate about fighting and is infamous for challenging any strong awakener. He used to be a brash loner and refused to take any disciples. After meeting Jiwoo, Kayden has become less harsh- although he still enjoys fighting, he doesn't challenge anyone recklessly. Kayden is also very confident often refers to himself in third person to emphasize his greatness.
